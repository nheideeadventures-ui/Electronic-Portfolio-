✨ Events Reflection

The different events in our school were really fun and memorable. It was nice to see how talented and creative the students were. Each activity had something unique, and it made the whole thing exciting for everyone.

The Noli Me Tangere play was very interesting because it helped bring the story to life. Instead of just reading it in the book, we were able to see the characters and scenes acted out on stage.

The VPOP event was very energetic and entertaining. All the sections sang and danced with confidence, and you could tell everyone worked really hard.

Booklandia was one of the most creative events. The contestants dressed up as different book characters and acted like them.

The Battle of the Bands was very exciting because the bands performed with a lot of energy and passion. LETS GOO GR 9 BANDS AND PADAYON AS 2026 BOTB CHAMP!

The Street Dance performances were also impressive. Even though my group didn't win, I’m grateful for the experience.

Lastly, the Miting de Avance reminded us how important it is to choose good leaders and vote wisely.
